Website Hosting

This website is hosted and delivered through Netlify, Inc. Netlify may process technical access data when the website is requested, including IP address, date and time of access, requested files, referrer URL, browser type, browser version and operating system information.

This processing is necessary to deliver the website, maintain security, troubleshoot errors and prevent abuse. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Our legitimate interest is the secure, reliable and efficient operation of this website.

Netlify may process data in the United States. Where required, transfers to third countries are based on appropriate safeguards, in particular standard contractual clauses or another transfer mechanism permitted under the GDPR.

Technical access data is stored only as long as necessary for delivery, security, troubleshooting and legal documentation, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

App Store Data and Media

The website displays public app information from the Apple App Store, including app names, descriptions, app icons and screenshots. App information is requested from Apple's public App Store interfaces and Apple-hosted media domains such as mzstatic.com.

App icons and screenshots are normally loaded through a Netlify image proxy operated for this website. In that case, your browser requests the image from this website, while the serverless function retrieves and caches the public image from Apple. Apple may receive technical request data from the server infrastructure used for the proxy. If the website is opened in a local/static fallback mode, App Store resources may also be requested directly from Apple by the browser.

The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Our legitimate interest is showing accurate, current App Store information for the apps listed on this website.

Serverless Functions and Image Cache

The website uses Netlify Functions to retrieve, normalize and cache public App Store data and media. These functions do not require a user account and are not used to create visitor profiles. Technical request data may be processed by Netlify as part of function execution, logging, caching, security and troubleshooting.

Cached app images are refreshed periodically so the website can stay up to date while reducing repeated requests to Apple and improving loading performance.

Cookies and Analytics

This website does not use analytics, advertising pixels or marketing cookies. No cookie consent banner is shown because no consent-requiring tracking is currently used.

Data Protection Officer

No data protection officer has been appointed. If the legal requirements for appointing a data protection officer become applicable, this privacy policy will be updated.

Contact by Email or Phone

If you contact us by email, we process your email address, message content and any metadata required to respond. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR if your message relates to a contract or app support request, and otherwise Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Messages are stored only as long as necessary for handling the request and legal retention obligations.

If you contact us by phone, we may process your phone number, call metadata and the content of your request to handle the communication. The same legal bases apply.

Requirement to Provide Data

Providing personal data is not legally required for browsing this website. Technical access data is generated automatically when the website is requested and is necessary to display the website. If you contact us, the information you provide is necessary to process and respond to your request.

Automated Decision-Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR on this website.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection where the legal requirements are met. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection supervisory authority.
