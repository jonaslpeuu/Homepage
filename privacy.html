Website Hosting

This website is hosted by Netlify, Inc. When you visit the website, technical access data may be processed, including IP address, date and time of access, requested files, referrer URL, browser information and operating system information. This processing is necessary to deliver the website securely and reliably. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Netlify may process data in the United States. Where required, data transfers are based on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

App Store Data and Media

The website displays app information, app icons and screenshots from Apple App Store sources, including Apple domains such as apps.apple.com and mzstatic.com. When these resources are loaded, Apple may receive technical access data from your browser. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR; our legitimate interest is presenting current App Store information for our apps.

Netlify Function

The website uses a serverless Netlify Function to retrieve and normalize public App Store data. The function does not require a user account and does not intentionally store visitor profiles.

Cookies and Analytics

This website does not use analytics, advertising pixels or marketing cookies. No cookie consent banner is shown because no consent-requiring tracking is currently used.

Email Contact

If you contact us by email, we process your email address, message content and any metadata required to respond. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR if your message relates to a contract or app support request, and otherwise Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR. Messages are stored only as long as necessary for handling the request and legal retention obligations.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability and objection where the legal requirements are met. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection supervisory authority.
